Connecting you to valuable resources within your company.

Need supplemental training for an upcoming project or just a refresher to update your existing skills? We match your training needs with co-worker mentors to create meaningful learning opportunities. Simply select a program, the desired skill level and receive a list of available mentors. Simply select one to engage and arrange training that fits your needs and both of your schedules. Learn directly from professionals already familiar with your company systems and culture and begin building your own reference network.

Matching you as the expert to co-workers with training needs.

Start sharing your knowledge and expertise with peers and co-workers. Inspire other porefessionals while advancing your own training skills and help your company build a community of shared learning. Update the skills and languages you can offer as you use the program and add your own new skills to help others. Manage when you are available to help and when your schedule allows to maximize time between projects.

Helping technology departments maximize training resources.

Every team has a way to address immediate challenges and long-term continuing Education. Sometimes however, your employees only need to fill training gaps for upcoming projects with a review or quick refresher. This progam empowers them to close that gap by connecting them to mentor resources already in your talent pool which leverages their available time, promotes employee engagement and enhances departmental development.
